Research Orientation
This portfolio sits at the intersection of systems thinking and empirical rigour — asking not just what is happening, but why structures persist, who benefits from the gap, and what intervention would most efficiently shift the equilibrium. Every proposal here begins where the consensus ends.

Research Philosophy
Every proposal in this portfolio is oriented around a well-defined gap — not a topic. The test applied at the ideation stage is simple: if the research were completed tomorrow, who would need to act differently, and why? Proposals that cannot answer this are returned to the drawing board, regardless of methodological elegance.
